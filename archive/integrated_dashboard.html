Methods & Limits

Data sources: SoilGrids SOC and TN mean predictions, WorldClim BIO1/BIO12 1970-2000 climate normals, MODIS MCD12C1 2021 IGBP land cover, and Koppen-Geiger climate classes.

SOC/TN are 0-30 cm thickness-weighted topsoil values. C:N = SOC / TN. High / medium / low SOC and TN are relative tercile classes from this project dataset.

Continuous map colors use the global 2nd-98th percentile range to reduce the influence of extreme values. Legend ranges are kept global for cross-filter comparability.

Current processed data do not include complete cell-level SoilGrids Q0.05-Q0.95 prediction intervals in the dashboard CSV. Therefore, the dashboard displays main SOC/TN predictions but does not show cell-level prediction intervals.

Uncertainty fields are retained in the dataset but are not used in the main visualizations because SOC/TN uncertainty layers are incomplete or unavailable.
