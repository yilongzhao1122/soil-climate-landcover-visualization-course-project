The files under `data/processed/` are included to make the dashboard reproducible and viewable:

- `grid_soc_tn_cn_0p5deg.csv`

## Source Data Terms

Before redistributing derived data for non-course or production use, verify the current license terms for:

- SoilGrids / ISRIC soil property products.
- WorldClim bioclimatic variables.
- Koppen-Geiger climate classification data.
- MODIS MCD12C1 land-cover data.
- Natural Earth boundaries.
